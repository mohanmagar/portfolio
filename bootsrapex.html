Welcome to SMC

Saptagandaki Multiple Campus (SMC), established in 1988 AD (2045 BS), is a community-based educational institution located in Bharatpur-10, Chitwan, Nepal. Affiliated with Tribhuvan University, the campus was founded through local efforts to provide quality and affordable education. It offers a wide range of academic programs, including 10+2 (Science, Management, Education, and Humanities), bachelor's degrees such as BBS, B.Ed, BA, BSc (Environmental Science), BIM, BBA, and BCA, and master's degrees like M.Ed, MBS, and MA in Rural Development. The institution currently serves over 4,000 students and employs more than 100 teaching staff. Known for its academic excellence and top-performing students, SMC has also been recognized by the University Grants Commission as one of the top public campuses.
